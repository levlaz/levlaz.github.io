This site is a combination of many other blogs that have been merged together over the years. Here’s the general timeline:

  • 2004 - 2005

    scraped together from a blog I had in high school along with importing some journal and bits from around the web. entries, this is missing a hundred other posts that were never archived and are gone forever. Probably a good riddance.

  • 2007 - 2012

    import from a defunct Blogger blog called “the thoughts bubble” that I wrote when I was in the navy. Tons of cringe poetry, political rants, diary entries. I once deleted everything but found it via web archive and I enjoy reading it now to remind me of how far I’ve come.

  • 2013 - 2023

    post-navy era, abandoned all dreams to pursue tech career, combination of blogs that bounced between jekyll, ghost, wordpress, hand rolled blog engine for a decade. Merged in tralev.net which was a travel blog that I wrote on my journey to all 50 state capitals.

  • December 2023 - Now

    rebirth as a hugo blog, hopefully the last migration.
